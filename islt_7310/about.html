My Resume

Hello there! I'm not your typical money guru, but with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and an Associate of Applied Science in Accounting, foundation to the world of personal finance. Now, I am on track to complete the Master of Business Administration program at the University of Missouri in July 2024.


The lack of money is the root of all evil. - Mark Twain

I love history and delving into the past. My natural curiosity allows me to use the past to interpret the present and I am always mindful of how the present will look when it becomes the past. I enjoy researching family traditions and learning how life was for the people who came before me. Some traditions are beautiful and I love to see them carry on. Others are harmful, and should be left in the past. We must break generational curses that are harmful and do not serve our goals. One of the most impactful ways to do this is by increasing financial literacy in your family or community. My passion is to help anyone who is “bad” with money learn how to manage their time and resources more effectively.
